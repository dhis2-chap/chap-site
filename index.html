    Chap & Modeling Portal

    Welcome to the online community for climate and health modeling and forecasting with DHIS2 and the Chap Modeling Platform

    A robust open-source platform for climate and health modeling

    Chap is a Climate & Health Modeling Platform that brings together climate health models into a unified ecosystem, connecting researchers and cutting-edge epidimiological models to policy makers and health practitioners. It makes complex modeling workflows accessible, streamlines rigorous model evaluation, and integrates directly with DHIS2, the world's leading health information system.

    Chap builds on and integrates with DHIS2 to support real-world use

    Chap Modeling Platform

    Python-based processing engine for model orchestration, data pipelines, validation, metrics, and hyperparameter tuning. It integrates seamlessly with DHIS2 system for accessing health data and disseminating forecasts, while also functioning as a standalone Python package and command-line tool.

    Get started

    DHIS2 Modeling App

    Pilot app for DHIS2 that provides seamless integration with the Chap Modeling Platform, providing a user-friendly interface within DHIS2 for selecting data to use to generate predictions in Chap – including data from the DHIS2 Climate App – train predictive models, visualize predictions, and evaluate model accuracy.

    Get started

    DHIS2 Climate App

    DHIS2 app to explore and import weather, climate, elevation, and other environmental data into DHIS2 data elements, making it available for use in the Chap Modeling Platform. The app uses several data sources, including ERA5-Land, considered to be the most accurate and complete climate dataset with worldwide coverage.
